About West Windsor-Plainsboro

What makes our community special?

Two townships. One community.

Within this beloved community, we aspire to create a united and harmonious family. Using this resource hub, our mission is to provide a comprehensive and user-friendly platform for residents of West Windsor-Plainsboro to access important information, resources, and services. Whether you're new to the area or have lived here for years, our goal is to help you stay informed and connected.

History

West Windsor-Plainsboro has a rich history that spans several decades. The community was established in the early 1900s and has grown to become a thriving place for families to live and raise their children. Although West Windsor and Plainsboro are separate municipalities, we are a unified community.

Community Diversity

West Windsor-Plainsboro is a vibrant, multicultural community that celebrates the diversity of its residents. We welcome people of all backgrounds and cultures, and encourage one another to share our unique customs.
