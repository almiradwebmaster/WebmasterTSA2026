Academic & Student Life [cite: 6]

The West Windsor-Plainsboro School District is defined by two friendly rivals: High School North and High School South. [cite: 8] Both share a commitment to educating children and building successful futures. [cite: 9] Each campus features unique traditions, clubs, and student-led initiatives. [cite: 10]

Science Olympiad [cite: 16]

Both HSN and HSS compete at the Division C Level, placing high in events across STEM disciplines. [cite: 17, 18] They are top contenders in NJ, boasting 6 championship titles. [cite: 19] North placed 1st in NJ finals in 2015, 2016, 2018, 2020, and 2023. [cite: 20]

Robotics [cite: 16, 22]

WWP North and South provide robust, hands-on robotics programs. [cite: 22] The MidKnight Inventors (FIRST Robotics Competition) focus on building 21st-century skills. [cite: 23] Learn more. [cite: 24]
